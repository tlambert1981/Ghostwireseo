## Getting Started

1. Install dependencies:
   ```bash
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   ```

2. Run the app:
   ```bash
   streamlit run streamlit_app.py
   ```

3. Set up your `.streamlit/secrets.toml`:
   ```toml
   RESEND_KEY = "your_resend_api_key"
   ```

## How It Works

1. **Select Your Industry** — Choose Legal, Medical, Construction, or Standard
2. **Enter Your Domain** — Input your website URL
3. **Set Your Position** — Slide your Google ranking (1-50)
4. **View Your Gap** — Instantly see your revenue opportunity
5. **Get Results** — Receive automated email with your analysis
6. **Unlock PDF** — Enter your Cash App $Cashtag to download your report

## Revenue Gap Methodology

Ghostwire Intelligence calculates your Revenue Gap by:
- Analyzing your Google position and market CPC data
- Comparing your current rank to top-performing competitors
- Computing potential monthly revenue based on search volume and conversion metrics
- Presenting industry-specific insights
